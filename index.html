About Me

I'm Henry Willems, and I have recently completed my master's in astrophysics at the University of Potsdam. I am an aspiring researcher in the field of astronomy and astrophysics. I am particularly interested in stellar astrophysics, exploring the life cycles, structure, and behavior of stars. My fields of interest also extend to galaxies, cosmology, and the search for exoplanets—fascinated by both the large-scale structure of the universe and the possibility of life beyond Earth.

I'm currently seeking PhD opportunities where I can contribute to cutting-edge research and grow as a scientist.
